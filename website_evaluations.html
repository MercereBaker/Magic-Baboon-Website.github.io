Website Evaluations

Japan National Tourism Oragnization - Hokkaido

This page, part of the JNTO website, shows off various features of Japan's Hokkaido prefecture for tourism.

    C.R.A.P. Design Analysis

  • Contrast - different font sizes for appropriate text, colorful pictures against whitespace, sections with different but similar margins.
  • Repetition - sans serif font, largely black text, consistent padding, similar sized images placed together.
  • Alignment - sections are organized in line grid systems, sections share the same margins or similar ones.
  • Proximity - sections covering different topics such as food specialties or attractions are segmented through headers, similar elements such as relevent pictures together.

The World's Worst Website Ever

An intentionally horrible website. The site is littered with inconsistent, tacky design and ads.

    C.R.A.P. Design Analysis

  • Contrast - the body background of paintings makes reading text difficult, text elements have different colored backgrounds, inconsistent and irrevelant font sizes
  • Repetition - diffrent fonts are all over the site, nonsensical images placed together, incompatible coloring
  • Alignment - there are no consistent, similar grid systems, weird margins
  • Proximity - elements that don't make sense are together such as Elon Musk's face and an irrelevant poll
